Online Safety

Protect accounts, avoid scams, and browse smart.

Protect Your Accounts

  • Use strong, unique passwords (12+ mixed characters).
  • Turn on two‑factor authentication (2FA).
  • Never share codes or recovery keys.

Avoid Scams & Malware

  • Be skeptical of unexpected money or gift card requests.
  • Don’t click unknown links or open shady attachments.
  • Install updates and reputable antivirus; keep them current.
  • Download software only from official sites/app stores.

Check Website Safety

  • Look for https:// and a lock icon.
  • Verify the domain (watch for misspellings).
  • Close pages full of deceptive pop‑ups.
