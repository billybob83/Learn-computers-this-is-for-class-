Computer Basics

Understand the parts and practice core skills.

Essential Parts

  • Monitor: Displays windows and apps.
  • Keyboard: Type text and use shortcuts (Ctrl+S to save).
  • Mouse/Touchpad: Move, click, right‑click, scroll.
  • Tower/Case: Processor, memory, storage live here.
  • Operating System: Windows, macOS, or Linux.

Choosing a Computer

  • Performance: 8–16 GB RAM and SSD suit most people.
  • Ports: USB‑A/USB‑C, HDMI/DisplayPort, headphone jack.
  • Form factor: Desktop for power; laptop for portability.
